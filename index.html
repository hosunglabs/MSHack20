HosungNotes X Jarvis Architecture

Users build solutions on HosungNotes based on Azure. HosungNotes made using the ASP.NET MVC5. The solutions builds up in MSSQL and downloads it into Word. After downloading, use the word file to train Azure Bot Jarvis. The training is based on Azure AI Service, QnAMaker.ai.

Therefore, users can always call in from the website of HosungNotes and from the Teams bot Jarvis to acquire solutions.

Next

What is Hosung-Notes?

It is a web program where you can save your solutions and find it easily.

First, there is a private ID, Password, in HosungNotes. If you don't have an ID or password, you can never share or find solutions.

Second, accurate search is possible. Find the Index of all DataBase and show the information.

Third, you can put tags, titles, and content in solutions. Tags are for anyone to easily find and study, and the title and content will be the content of the Case task. In addition, content can be copied and pasted easily, and any typical Editor function is available.

What is the Jarvis bot?

Jarvis is an Azure Bot program that works with Teams.

Jarvis has added Azure QnAMaker.ai, an Azure Cognitive Service. This made it easy for Jarvis to learn from the solutions in HosungNotes.

In addition, Jarvis includes lunch or get-together locations, monthly events, company-required URLs, and club information.

Next

Meet HosungNotes' solutions in the Microsoft Teams.

You can easily find it in Teams using Jarvis, who has learned HosungNotes through Teams. You can also call Jarvis directly on the Teams channel to check and discuss Jarvis' answers with the team members.

Next

Jarvis is using Azure Cognitive Service, QnAMaker.ai.

Click the brain icon and attach the solutions received in Wordfiles from HosungNotes to Azure Cognitive Service QnAMaker.ai then AI will complete questions and answers automatically. Therefore, Jarvis can be learned quickly and easily and can be used.

HosungNotes X Jarvis

The combination of HosungNotes and Jarvis represents a more powerful advantage.

Build your solutions easily

It is easy to accumulate, modify, and erase solutions, also easy to educate the bot through QnAMaker.ai

Get your solutions from Teams

Get case solution information from the Teams Channel using Jarvis. This solution can also be solved by working with other team members. Use this to increase the accuracy of troubleshooting

There are no time and platform constraints

Jarvis works in Teams. Teams is also available on mobile. HosungNotes works on the Web, so you can choose what's comfortable. It also operates 24 hours a day, so there is no time limit

Save the document your solutions

Download your solutions by Word file with one click and train Jarvis Teams bot

Amazing Scalability

With Azure VM, you can create your own HosungNotes and Jarvis.
Jarvis can also be used in Teams, Slack, Web, Skype, and more

Without authority cannot see your solutions

Access to HosungNotes is only available to people with shared ID and passwords. Jarvis bot is also designed to be certified using the confidential channel and authorization API
